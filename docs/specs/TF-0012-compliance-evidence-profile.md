# TF-0012: Compliance Evidence Profile

## Status

Draft.

## Non-claim

TrustForge does not automatically make any organization compliant.

## Evidence goals

TrustForge should help prove who acted, what actor instance acted, what authority they had, what policy allowed/denied the action, what approval was given, whether quorum was met, what system executed the action, what proof was generated, when it happened, and whether the event chain is tamper-evident.

## Evidence modes

- session evidence
- action evidence
- approval evidence
- policy-decision evidence
- payload commitment evidence
- encrypted evidence bundle
- notarized proof
- external anchor

## Relevant domains

- MSPs
- healthcare
- finance
- government
- maritime
- critical infrastructure
- remote support
- enterprise AI
- device management
- firmware control
